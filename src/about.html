Map About

What is this?

skateMap is a free tool for the Newcastle Skate Scene to find skate spots and parks near them. Browse the map to discover spots across Newcastle and the surrounding area, tap any marker to read about the spot, and use the Find Me button to centre the map on your current location.

How to use it

Zoom and pan the map to explore spots across the city. Tap any purple marker to open a popup with the spot name and description. Hit the Find Me button to see where you are relative to the nearest spots.

The story

I built this site because I wanted to create a deployable project while I am studying Computer Science. The idea came from wanting to build something genuinely useful for the local scene while also keeping me interested enough to actually finish it.

The goal is to keep building it out over time, adding features the community actually wants.
